Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is pyRS - Python Reporting Server?

    2. pyRS is an application that will allow you to distribute your IPython notebooks throughout your organization. You have the ability to re-run notebooks or simply share your analysis via a web url.

  2. How to add/remove a notebook in BARS?

    3. All of the notebooks are housed in a git repository.

  3. How to share your notebook via a web url?

    4. pyRS provides links to the notebook you can use to share your analysis.

  4. How to re-run your notebook?

    5. By clicking on the update button, your notebook will be re-run and saved back to the repository.
